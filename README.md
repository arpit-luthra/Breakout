## 🕹️ Breakout 

**Summary**
---
Breakout is a classic C# arcade game featuring dynamic ball physics, multi-hit bricks, and progressive speed scaling. Built with .NET, it challenges players to clear multiple screens while managing interactive power-ups.

**Objective**
---
Break all bricks to win. As you progress, the ball speeds up and your paddle shrinks—collect Plus signs for firing abilities and Hearts for extra lives to complete all levels.

**Instructions & Controls**
---
**Move**: Left/Right Arrows (Up-Arrow to launch ball)

**Power-ups**: Plus (+): Firing/Support Paddles, Heart: Extra Life, Triangle: Slows mobility

**Pause** / Exit: P to Pause, Esc to Exit (from Main Menu)

**Menu**: Backspace to return to the main menu

**How to Run**
---
* Download and extract the ZIP.
* Open GalaxyGuardian.sln in Visual Studio.
* Press Start to play.
